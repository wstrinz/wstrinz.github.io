AHM Check

Authentic Hopeful Material score: a subjective read on whether a candidate can carry a positive national story under sustained, low-filter exposure.

The test combines five traits: positive vision, clear-eyed problem naming, selective fight-picking, comfort being themselves in long-form settings, and material specifics voters can actually point to.

This is not a competence or coalition-building score; those should mostly show up in polling and markets. It is a soft signal for what may become more visible as the primary heats up.

4 = natural carrier. 3 = credible with a weakness. 2 = partial fit. 1 = brand fights the thesis.
