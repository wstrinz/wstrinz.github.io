Vibes Check

Derived Vibes Check score: a weighted read on whether a candidate can carry a positive national story under sustained, low-filter exposure.

The stored data keeps five 1-4 ingredients: hope story, material specificity, authentic longform, problem clarity, and fight selection. The Cycle lens control changes only the weighting, not the stored scores.

Functionally, this is a candidate-authorship score: how much the candidate, rather than the campaign apparatus, seems to generate the campaign's story and substance.

This is not a competence or coalition-building score; those should mostly show up in polling and markets. It is a soft signal for what may become more visible as the primary heats up.

4 = natural carrier. 3 = credible with a weakness. 2 = partial fit. 1 = brand fights the thesis.

Mention Test

Manual captures label each mention as good, bad, neutral, or unclear, with source/ref kept next to the count. Good-mention share and volume are tracked separately.

The hypothesis is tested on lag: if better mentions precede later market or poll movement, the signal is useful. If mentions only move after markets move, it is descriptive rather than predictive.
