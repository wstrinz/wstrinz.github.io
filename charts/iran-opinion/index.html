🎯 TL;DR — Tanvir Was Right
Not a protest movement
The "opposition" is mostly Democrats reflexively opposing Trump, not a genuine antiwar constituency. Independents lean against but aren't passionate.
Nobody loves Iran's leadership
The "was it justified to kill Khamenei?" question is nearly even — Americans don't feel sorry for the regime. The objection is strategic, not sympathetic.
