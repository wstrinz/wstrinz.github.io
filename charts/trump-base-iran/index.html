🐘 Trump's Republican Base: Iran vs. Other Issues

Republican approval of Trump's handling by policy area · Mar 2026 · Hover for source notes

✅ Base Holding on Iran (Air War)

At ~85%, Republican approval of Iran strikes is within normal range for Trump's core supporters — similar to immigration and well above his overall approval (~40%). The base is aligned on the air war.

⚠️ The Real Crack: Ground Troops

52% of Republicans oppose sending ground troops into Iran — the one issue where Trump's base visibly diverges. The air war is fine; a land war is where the coalition starts fracturing.

📊 Tariffs: The Hidden Weakness

Tariffs are the soft underbelly of Republican approval. YouGov finds majorities of Americans want lower tariffs, and Republican support is lower than on Iran or immigration — especially among business-aligned GOP voters.

⚠️ Data transparency: Iran War Republican approval (~85%) is from Quinnipiac/Fox News polls (sourced). Immigration/border (~90%) and economy handling (~78%) are from CBS/YouGov crosstabs (sourced). DOGE/spending cuts (~79%) and tariffs (~68%) are from YouGov topline data with Republican crosstab estimates — directionally accurate but treat as approximate.
